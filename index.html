My Journey

Early Days

Started healthy at home but gradual weight gain unnoticed till 85, then 90, 95, 98 kg.

College Life

Hostel life increased laziness, physical inactivity, reaching 98 kg. Waist size 36, health issues including disc pain and constipation.

Turning Point

Failed multiple plans, skipped gym despite paying fees. Finally decided to transform self without gym.

Transformation

Started gradually, running, strict diet, and consistent effort. Now able to run 5 km daily and lost significant weight.

Tips for Readers

  4. Mindset aur motivation sabse important hai.
