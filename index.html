Meri Weight Loss Journey

WITHOUT GYM

Just Self Dedication and hard work.

98 kg se 60 kilogram– Apni mehnat aur dedication se

Introduction

Mera starting weight 98 kg tha. Main decide kiya ki bina gym ke aur sirf dedication se apni health aur life transform karunga.

Journey

  • Diet: Balanced aur portion-controlled meals, ghar ke healthy khane.
  • Exercise: Walking, home workouts, stretching aur consistency.
  • Mindset: Patience, motivation aur consistency sabse important tha.

Challenges

Cravings aur slow progress challenges the. Lekin maine patience aur discipline se handle kiya.

Results & Transformation

Maine 38 kg lose kiye! Health better hui, confidence boost hua, aur life totally change ho gayi.

Tips for Readers

  1. Consistency aur dedication rakho.
  2. Small steps start karo, realistic goals set karo.
  3. Patience rakho, slow progress bhi success hai.
